Healthy Chicken Muffin

Description:

If you're looking for a balanced and easy to make meal, this is for you.
It might not look very good, but it's tasty and healthy!


Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 60g shredded chicken
  • 40ml milk
  • 3g chemical yeast
  • 35g light ricotta cream
  • 40g oat

How to make:

  1. Combine milk, eggs and ricotta cream in a bowl until it turns into a full blended mixture.

  2. Add oat, shredded chicken and mix it together.

  3. Add yeast to the mix, and you're ready to bake it.

  4. Transfer the mixture into a silicone mold of your choice.

  5. Bake it for 15 minutes in an preheated oven at 200ÂºC, or you can put it into a a microwave and heat it for 3 to 4 minutes.
